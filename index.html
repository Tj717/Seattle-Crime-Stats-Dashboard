Seattle crime rate graduatiuly increase over the year. In 2018, the rate was about 680 occurances for every 100,000 population. Later the number jumped up towards the 700s. In 2021, the rate was 729 occurances per 100,000. The following year, 2022, rates increased to 736 per 100,000.
